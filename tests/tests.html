Body Copy

Golden's global default font-size is 14px, with a line-height of 1.618. This is applied to the <body> and all paragraphs. In addition, <p> (paragraphs) receive a bottom margin of half their computed line-height (11px by default).

Buttons

The height of the default button is related to the line height by the golden ratio: buttonHeight = lineHeight*goldenRatio. The amount of space above and below the text is related to the base font size by the golden ratio: top + bottom = fontSize*goldenRatio. Where top = botttom = ((lineHeight-fontSize)/2) + (paddingVertical/2). Also buttonHeight = fontSize*goldenRatio*goldenRatio

The height of the large button. The height of the large button is related to the height of the regular size button by: largeButtonHeight = 2*regularButtonHeight/goldenRatio. The height of the large button is alse related to the text size of the font inside the large button by the golden ratio. buttonHeight = fontSize*goldenRatio*goldenRatio.

The height of the small button. The height of the small button is related to the height of the regular size button by: smallButtonHeight = 2*regularButtonHeight*(1-(1/goldenRatio)). The height of the large button is alse related to the text size of the font inside the large button by the golden ratio. buttonHeight = fontSize*goldenRatio*goldenRatio.

The height of the xs button. The height of the extra small button is related to the height of the regular sized button by the golden ratio: xsmallButtonHeight = defaultButtonHeight/goldenRatio.

NOTE:: Buttons should be placed under .golden-typography

Fancy larger or smaller buttons? Add .btn-lg, .btn-sm, or .btn-xs for additional sizes.
