Getting Started .golden

All of Golden is rooted under the .golden class. It applies a bunch of awesome styles, but some can be unpredictable. For example, it changes the box model.

Typography

Headings

All HTML headings, <h1> through <h6>, are available. .h1 through .h6 classes are also available, for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but still want your text to be displayed inline.

Body Copy

Golden's global default font-size is 14px, with a line-height of 1.618. This is applied to the <body> and all paragraphs. In addition, <p> (paragraphs) receive a bottom margin of half their computed line-height (11px by default).
